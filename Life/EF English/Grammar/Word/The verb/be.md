# Be

## be动词的三种形式

> be动词有三种形式，am are is

#### am 与人称代词 I搭配

I am Kevin

#### is搭配人称代词 it she he

He is a student.

She is  a teacher.

It is new.

#### are 搭配 you they we,人称代词you 可做单数，也可做复数

We are students.

You are student.

You are students.

They are students.

## be 动词：缩写

I'm kevin

He's a student.

She's a teacher.

It's new.

You're a student.

We're new.

They're students.
